About

"I speak fluent art! - Let me translate your ideas!
Do you speak art? Let's speak together!

My name is Sofie Florczak. I am 25 years old and a multimedia design student from Odense, Denmark.
I am also an artist, and my biggest passion is to create all kinds of art. It has been a lifelong hobby which I hope I can turn into a lifelong profession.
I have always been very creative, and started drawing when I could hold a pencil.
Gradually I began experimenting with different mediums and styles of drawing and painting.
I also enjoy photography and discovered the world of digital art, and the endless possibilities of what you can create - this sparked the idea of studying multimedia design.
I enjoy making posters, logos, cover-designs, illustrations, even tattoo designs and giving new digital life to old drawings.
What gives me joy is to realize and communicate other people’s ideas visually and give them my own twist.
I like working with all kinds of subjects, since I am very curious by heart, and always strive to learn new things, which gives fuel to the creative thinking process.
I feel most at ease when I am using my hands for drawing or painting physically, but I want to expand my knowledge and skills with digital methods, merging the two together,since modern technology keeps on expanding and is an important part of this era.
I’m in the zone when I can ping-pong with other dedicated, creative and skilled people, while at the same time being able to immerse myself in my own work or projects.
Through time I've done different commissions for family, friends and friends of friends etc. and it is my dream to one day be able to work freelance with a wide spectrum of projects!
